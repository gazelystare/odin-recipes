Chana Masala and Brown Rice

A mildly spicy Indian dish made with chickpeas, tomatoes, traditional spices, and brown rice.

Ingredients:

  • Onion (1)
  • Head of garlic, sliced (1)
  • Can of chickpeas (1)
  • 28 oz. can of diced tomatoes (1)
  • Garam masala, 1 tsp
  • Kosher salt, 1 tsp
  • Garlic powder, 1 tsp
  • Parsley, 1/2 cup
  • Brown rice, cooked, 4 cups

Steps:

  1. Chop the onion and garlic.
  2. Saute in olive oil for 5 mins.
  3. Add chickpeas and spices and simmer 5 mins.
  4. Add diced tomatoes and simmer for 10 mins.
  5. When done, stir in parsley and remove from heat.
  6. Serve over brown rice.
