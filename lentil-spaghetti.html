Lentil Spaghetti

A protein-packed plant-based alternative to the traditional dish.

Ingredients:

  • Onion (1)
  • Head of garlic, sliced (1)
  • Cooked lentils, 2 cups
  • 28 oz. can of diced tomatoes (1)
  • Italian seasoning, 1 tsp
  • Kosher salt, 1 tsp
  • Garlic powder, 1 tsp
  • Spinach, 1/2 cup
  • Spaghetti noodles, 4 cups

Steps:

  1. Chop the onion and garlic.
  2. Saute in olive oil for 5 mins.
  3. Add diced tomatoes and spices and simmer for 10 mins.
  4. When done, stir in spinach and lentils and remove from heat.
  5. Serve over spaghetti noodles.
