Chickpea Salad

A flavorful and aromatic quick lunch option.

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil, 3 Tbs
  • Lemon juice, 3 Tbs
  • Garlic cloves, chopped, 4
  • Sea salt, 1/2 tsp
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved, 1 1/2 cups
  • Cucumber, halved and sliced, 1
  • Chickpeas, 1 can, drained and rinsed
  • Red onion, 1/2, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Cilantro, chopped, 1/4 cup

Steps:

  1. Combine the dressing ingredients in a small bowl: olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and salt, and whisk to combine.
  2. Combine remaining ingredients in a salad bowl, add dressing and toss to coat.
