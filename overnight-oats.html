Overnight Oats

A creamy plant-based breakfast on-the-go.

Ingredients:

  • Soymilk, 1 cup
  • Oats, 1/2 cup
  • Maple syrup, 2 tsp
  • Chia seeds, 2 Tbs
  • Fruit and granola, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingredients in mason jar.
  2. Shake vigorously.
  3. Refrigerate 2-24 hours, and eat within 4 days.
